# Course Browser

**Who:** A learner or educator scanning the registry to find courses worth loading into the desktop app.
**Tech level:** Non-technical to junior developer -- comfortable using a browser but not building software.
**Patience:** Low -- will leave if they can't find something relevant within a few seconds.

## What they care about
- Finding courses that match their interest quickly
- Trusting that listed courses are legitimate and functional
- Getting from the registry into the desktop app with minimal steps

## How they approach the app
- Skims titles and descriptions, rarely clicks through unless something looks promising
- Uses search and tag filters before scrolling through pages
- Expects the "Open in app" flow to just work -- treats the registry as a launcher, not a destination

## What would frustrate them
- Search results that feel irrelevant or unsorted
- No way to gauge course quality before committing (missing descriptions, no load counts, no tags)
- Friction between finding a course and actually using it in the desktop app
