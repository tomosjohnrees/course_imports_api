# Desktop App User

**Who:** Someone using the Course Imports desktop app who encounters the registry via deep links or in-app browsing.
**Tech level:** Varies -- could be anyone from a student to a developer, but interacts with the registry passively.
**Patience:** Very low -- they are mid-task in the desktop app and any registry friction breaks their flow.

## What they care about
- Deep links working reliably to open courses directly in their app
- Course metadata being accurate so they know what they are importing
- The registry loading fast since it is a waypoint, not a destination

## How they approach the app
- Arrives from the desktop app or a shared link, lands on a course detail page
- Clicks "Open in app" and expects the handoff to succeed immediately
- Rarely browses the registry directly -- treats it as infrastructure behind the desktop app

## What would frustrate them
- Deep links that fail silently or open a blank page
- Stale metadata that doesn't match what actually loads in the desktop app
- Being forced to sign in just to view or import an approved course
