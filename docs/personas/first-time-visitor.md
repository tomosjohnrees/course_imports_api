# First-Time Visitor

**Who:** Someone who landed on the site from a link, search engine, or word of mouth — has no idea what this app is or how it relates to the desktop app.
**Tech level:** Varies — could be anyone from a non-technical learner to an experienced developer.
**Patience:** Very low — will bounce in seconds if the purpose isn't immediately clear.

## What they care about
- Understanding what this site is and whether it's relevant to them
- Figuring out the relationship between the registry and the desktop app
- Deciding whether to sign up, browse, or leave

## How they approach the app
- Reads the landing page looking for a value proposition before doing anything else
- Won't sign in with GitHub unless they understand why
- Judges legitimacy by visual polish and clarity — if it looks half-built, they assume it's abandoned

## What would frustrate them
- No clear explanation of what the app does on the first screen
- Being asked to authenticate before understanding the value
- Jargon or assumed context about the desktop app, course format, or workflow
